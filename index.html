Umrah With the Sunnah
Shower
Step 1

Taking a bath/shower as one would from sexual relations and men apply fragrance. Women must not apply fragrance or scent.

Ihram
Step 2

Wearing the garments of Ihrām, the izār and ridā (upper and lower cloth). The woman may wear whatever she choose under her jilbāb (but she cannot tie the niqāb or wear gloves).

Niyyah
Step 3

The utterance for entering ihrām at the appointed meeqāt. Then one recites the talbiyah often until he is about to begin the Tawāf.

Tawaf
Step 4

To make seven circuits of the Ka’bah (Tawāf) beginning at the Black Stone (al-Hajr al-Aswad) and ending at the Black Stone.

Offering Salah
Step 5

Praying the two rak’ahs behind the Station of Ibrāheem.

Sa'ee
Step 6

The Sa’ee between Mount Safa and Mount Marwah of seven circuits, beginning at Safā and finishing at Marwah.

Haircut
Step 7

Shaving or trimming the hair for men. Shortening the hair for women.
